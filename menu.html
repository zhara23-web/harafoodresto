Customer Reviews

We greatly value every review and feedback from our customers. Your reviews help us continually improve the quality of our products and services. Thank you for sharing your experience with us!

Fitria

I recently visited Harafood, and it was an amazing experience. From the moment I walked in, the ambiance was cozy and inviting. The staff were incredibly friendly and made me feel right at home. I tried the Nasi Goreng, and it was one of the best I’ve ever had—perfectly cooked rice, just the right amount of spice, and the fried egg on top was a great touch. The Lychee Rose Sparkle drink was a refreshing and unique choice, and I loved the subtle floral notes. Definitely a must-visit!

Karina

I love Harafood for its relaxed, casual vibe. It’s the perfect place to enjoy a meal with friends after a long day. The Ayam Geprek Sambal Bawang is a must-try—the fried chicken is crispy, and the sambal is the right amount of spicy. Their Nasi Ayam Bakar Kecap is another standout, with tender chicken and the sweet, savory taste of soy sauce. The staff is always welcoming, and the food is served promptly. I highly recommend this place for anyone craving delicious and comforting food.

Alwi

Harafood truly offers a taste of Indonesia in every bite. I tried the Sop Buntut and it was hands down the best I’ve ever had. The oxtail was tender, and the broth was rich and flavorful. My friend had the Gado-Gado, which was fresh and crunchy, with a creamy peanut sauce that was to die for. The staff were helpful and gave great recommendations. I also love the fact that they offer a variety of beverages that pair well with each dish. Harafood is definitely a place I’d recommend to anyone craving authentic Indonesian cuisine
